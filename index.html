AMI AUTO SPA

AMI Auto Spa, locally owned and operated, was established in 2015 with a dream, a vision and a passion for the auto detailing & reconditioning industry. We pride ourselves on outstanding customer service and maintain the highest level of quality and dedication. Each vehicle is treated with utmost pride and integrity. We cater exclusively to the individual auto owner's needs, wants and desires. Our performance is reflected on each and every final product that we deliver.


We pride ourselves on being a service-oriented company willing to tackle any project that comes our way. We excel in custom painting and custom airbrush work, design, graphics, flames, pin striping, murals, color matching, bodywork, and more.


Everything we do is designed to help you make the best impression possible. We use brilliant, top of the line products, along with premium materials, state-of-the-art technologies, and a host of imaginative ideas to create the kind of image you'll be proud to display.


We certainly enjoy detailing all types of vehicles, but when it comes to your toys, we are the premier shop in the area to take care of your investment.


If there is anything we can do to make this experience more enjoyable or anything that our company can change to help you in any way please let us know. Thank you for your interest in our company and we look forward to working with you.

SERVICES

What we offer


WASH TREATMENTS/DETAILING

Full interior steam and cleaning, carpet, floor mat, seat belt, headliner, leather treatment, console, door panel, windows, exterior wash and hand wax.

CUSTOM LIGHTS

We install single or multi-color SMD, standard, or expandable LEDs and feature a variety of distinct lighting effects that will give your vehicle a custom, one of a kind look.

FABRICATION

We can install a very nice state-of-the-art hot match pop-up gas cap, aircraft style or a cool sport bike style in any gas tank. We can install any of these cool looking flush caps. You want your ride to be unique and stylin'? These gas caps are not like the cheap imports (that do break).



CUSTOM TAIL LIGHTS

Looking to customize your motorcycle tail lights? We create recessed LED tail lights to give your motorcycle a unique look. Custom painted design incorporating the tail lights will definitely add style and get your motorcycle noticed.

CUSTOM PAINT JOBS

We specialize in custom painting motorcycles, with any scheme and design you have in mind. We can take any idea you have and create a unique look, that no one else has. Our motorcycle custom paint designs range from Mild to Wild, with endless options and amazing detail.

REPAIR SERVICES

If you damage your motorcycle, bicycle, car or truck, we are the place to come. In addition to a top notch skills in bodywork, paint and repair our crerw understands custom paint and exotic colors and product. If you have a custom paint job or a limited addition OEM paint, we can match and repair it. We can reverse engineer colors that can not be matched by camera or formula and we understand how to apply products to match a look and make a minor or major mishap disappear.
